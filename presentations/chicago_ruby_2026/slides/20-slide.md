# Pitcher throws the ball

Batter tries to hit the ball and run around the bases

Infield Fly Rule (batter is automatically "out" on a popup with runners on 1st and 2nd or bases loaded)

Foul tip is a strike… unless it's the third strike

A "strike" is a pitch over the plate

A "ball" is a pitch that is not a strike

A "strike" is any pitch that is swung at & missed
