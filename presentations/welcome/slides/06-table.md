## GFM tables

| Theme       | Vibe                          | Best for              |
|-------------|-------------------------------|-----------------------|
| `minimal`   | Whitespace, big sans          | Most talks            |
| `editorial` | Serif headings, accent rules  | Storytelling          |
| `terminal`  | Mono, blinking cursor         | Devops, CLI demos     |
| `aurora`    | Gradient text, soft glow      | Product launches      |

Switch themes by editing `config.yml`.
